Our Story

A Sanctuary
Rooted in Bali

Philosophy

Founded in 2008, The Damai was born from a singular vision: to create a place where guests could reconnect with the island's ancient rhythms. Every decision — from the architecture to the cuisine — honours Bali's living culture.

Our 12 private villas are nestled across 4 hectares of terraced jungle. No two are alike. Crafted by local artisans using reclaimed teak, volcanic stone, and hand-painted tiles, each space tells a different story.

Our Values

Sustainability

Solar-powered, rainwater harvesting, and an on-site organic garden supply over 60% of our kitchen's needs.

Community

Over 90% of our team are from the local villages. We support traditional craft schools and cultural ceremonies year-round.

Authenticity

No two days are the same. From private offerings ceremonies at dawn to cooking classes with village elders.
